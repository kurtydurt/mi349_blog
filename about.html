About Sam Kurtzman

Sam Kurtzman has been a devoted listener to Hip Hop for about as long as he can remember. He is also the host of Impact 88.9FM's Hip-Hop, RnB, Funk, and Soul specialty show: The Vibe, hosted from 8 - 12PM on Saturday night. When he isn't listening to music you can likely find him wandering local nature trails or burying his head in whatever book he is reading at the moment. He insists on only reading one at a time.
